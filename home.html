We offer a variety of services to provide you and your horse the best fit possible, with most of our work done on-site in our mobile workshop.
Our services include:
  • Sales of new and pre-owned saddles
  • Saddle reflocking
  • Replacing billets
  • Adjusting tree width on brands we sell
  • Adjusting flocking
  • Minor repairs
Julie Welch is a Member of the Society of Master Saddlers and has been an equestrienne since 1985. A midwest native, she trained in England on the proper way to fit saddles and bridles, and started her own business in 2023 to share her skills with other riders.
